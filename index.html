Do you want to learn how to dance?

Learn how to dance at the Fiesta Dance School, based in Basingstoke. Our IDTA qualified dance instructors offer classes in a wide range of dance styles, including Latin American, Salsa and Ballroom. Our classes are tailored towards beginner and advanced dancers, so it doesn’t matter what level of skill you are, everyone is welcome to participate. To ensure we meet your needs, as well as group tuition, we also provide private lessons.

What We Offer

  • Wide range of dances
  • Beginners and advanced classes
  • Professional teacher training to IDTA qualification
  • Social and medalist dancing
  • Group and private tuition

About Us

Fiesta Dance School was founded in 1960 and since then it has grown to be comprised of 5 talented dance teachers, with many years of experience between them, 4 of which are IDTA qualified. Fiesta dance lessons take place in three different locations: the Moose Centre in Basingstoke, Victoria Hall in Hartley Wintney and the Memorial Hall in Pamber Heath.

Our teachers

Peni Harman Peni has passed her student teacher Latin exam and is an IDTA approved dance teacher. Peni has been dancing for 36 years and continues to further her qualifications in Ballroom, Latin, Freestyle and Rock & Roll dance.

Kerry PrettyKerry started dancing when she was 10 years old and since then has gone on to pass various exams, including her student ballroom teacher exam, and has also become IDTA approved. Kerry enjoys teaching dance to people of all ages.

Joe Harman Joe is an IDTA qualified dance teacher with more than 45 years of dancing experience, 30 of which were spent teaching other dancers to become professional qualified teachers who run their own dance schools and classes.

Donna Stoyle Donna began dancing at the Fiesta school as a child and has gone on to pass many dancing exams, including her IDTA teacher exams. Donna enjoys teaching Freestyle and Rock & Roll dance at the Fiesta Dance School.

Jessica Bone Jessica has been dancing with Fiesta since she was 5 years old and passed her first proffesional exam in December of 2011. With a passion for ballroom and Latin dancing, Jessica assists Fiesta Dance School teachers with adult classes.
